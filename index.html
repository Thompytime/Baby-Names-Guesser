How to Play

Guess the baby name in 5 tries.

Each guess must be a valid name. Hit the enter button or click Submit to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the name.

Rules

  • You have 5 attempts to guess the correct baby name
  • Names are from the top 100 boys' names in England & Wales 2024
  • You can guess any name, not just ones from the list
  • Green tiles indicate correct letters in correct positions
  • Yellow tiles indicate correct letters in wrong positions
  • Grey tiles indicate letters not in the name
  • Each letter can only be counted once in the target name
  • A new name appears every day at midnight
  • Your stats and streaks are saved locally in your browser
